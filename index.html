How to use this on phone → big TV

On your phone, open this page, hit TV Mode, then cast via AirPlay / Chromecast / HDMI adapter. (TV Mode also works great if you’re just mirroring your screen.)

Tap a card to play in-page. Esc closes the player. On desktop, use ←/→ to jump between videos while the player is open.
